Crypto-Fiends

What is Crypto-Fiends?

Crypto-Fiends is a game of chance that can make you money. There are 900+ unique stat combinations for each unit in the game, and only 1 copy of any unit. Be an owner of a completely unique card!

How do you play Crypto-Fiends?

You need 4 Crypto-Fiends to play. Each Crypto-Fiend in the game has a fiend-specific ability. Crypto-Fiends come in 4 different sizes. Each Crypto-Fiend has health, damage, accuracy, and an attribute that boosts a stat. The first team of 4 to defeat the enemy team wins. Higher level and rarity fiends are stronger. Win matches, collect tickets to buy exclusive fiends. Collect and sell your fiends for real money.
